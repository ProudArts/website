Privacy Policy — Proud Arts LLC

Last updated: 2025-08-31

Proud Arts does not collect, store, or process personal data directly from players of our games.

All account, purchase, and activity data are handled by the platform you use (for example, Epic Games, Steam, PlayStation, Xbox, or Nintendo) under that platform’s own terms and privacy policy.

Our games may use platform features such as sign-in, ownership/entitlement checks, achievements/trophies, overlays, or cloud saves. Any personal information involved in those features is processed by the platform. Proud Arts does not receive platform passwords or payment card information.
