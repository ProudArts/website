How to use: Compare vehicles of the same make, model, or year. This tool calculates the cost for every mile left in the vehicle's expected life.

Method: We subtract current mileage from the estimated lifespan (default 200,000) to find remaining miles, then divide price by those miles. The vehicle with the lowest cost per mile will be highlighted in green.
