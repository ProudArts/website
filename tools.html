Vehicle Remaining Mile Calculator

Cost per Remaining Mile
-

How it works: Most modern vehicles are expected to last approximately 200,000 miles. This calculator helps you determine the "unit price" of a vehicle's remaining life.

By subtracting the current mileage from 200,000, we find the estimated miles left. Dividing the price by these miles reveals your cost per mile, allowing you to compare two similar vehicles objectively.

Example: A lower-priced car with significantly higher mileage might actually cost you more "per mile" than a more expensive car with fewer miles.
