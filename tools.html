How to use: Compare vehicles of the same make, model, or year. This tool calculates the "unit price" of a vehicle's remaining life based on a 200,000-mile benchmark.

Method: We subtract current mileage from the estimated lifespan to find remaining miles, then divide price by those miles. If a vehicle is built to last longer (like a diesel truck), adjust the lifespan in Advanced Settings.
